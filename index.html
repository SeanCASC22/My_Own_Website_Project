About me!

I take my time to thoroughly learn any concept instead of trying to quickly grasp them.
I like being the lone wolf, soloing nearly all my personal projects (like the ones here)!
While I am insightful and a great listener, I don't do well under stress and pressure.
I'm a Pisces with a personality of INFJ-T who has a passion for writing and art!
You can say I'm philosophical, I have a mind that leans towards symbolism.
For a while I've been trying to learn more about art and psychology.
Usually I believe in what everyone else would never believe.
My favorite color is purple and Spring is my favorite season!
My birthday is February 20.

My skills

Not only am I an avid reader who can read quickly, but I can also kind of read people.
I have the ability to know without thinking because of my intuition.
I've nearly perfected most forms of literature you can think of.
My creativity at its best is nearly unparalleled. I'm original.
As an empath, I can feel what anyone else feels.
I know how to visualize extremely well.
I go all out when I'm fully inspired!

My ambitions

Since I want to learn more about who I am, I'd like to find out more about myself.
Enchancing my skill in literature could be a good idea!
Prioritize bonding with the ones who are close to me.
I want to improve my artistic skill as much as I can!
I'd like to write my own books one day.
What if I created my own songs?
